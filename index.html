What's the Four Degrees Movement?

It all starts from the six degrees of separation - a theory that says any person is six or fewer steps away from any other person in the world. Recently, top scientists have analyzed social network graphs from Facebook and Twitter, concluding that the true degree of separation is actually only four.

The caveat is, many of these connections are superficial, such as the person who liked your Facebook status, or retweeted one of your posts. Our mission is to bring these connections to life - one traveler at a time - by providing the platform to turn these introductions into real friendships.

Save Planning Time.
Studies show that many frequent travelers spend up to two days a year just at the planning stage. Whether it be figuring out transportation details or the optimal hotel to stay at, it can really be a nightmare dealing with all the possible options.

With a local at your destination by your side - both through online video communication and in real life - you can have your plans taken care of, in the most painless way possible.

Explore like a local. Falling into tourist traps is one of the worst parts about traveling, but it can be difficult to figure out where the local hotspots are.

With a real local showing you around, you can get a feel for what it truly means live there, immerse yourself in the culture around you, and make your travel experience truly worth it.

Make friends globally. Great conversations start from a drink, a little free time, and the willingness to open ourselves up. Our community of travelers and guides truly care about deep personal connections and long-lasting friendships, independent of your location.
